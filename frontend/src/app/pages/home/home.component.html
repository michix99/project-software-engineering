Welcome to our Prototype of a Correction Management System!

This web application provides you a correction management system for helping to improve teaching materials.

On the left you will find the menu which will enable you to navigate through the system.
The following pages are currently available:

  • Home - The start page of the application.
  • Create Ticket - The editor to create a new correction request.
  • Ticket Overview - The list of all correction requests for admins and editors. The list of own correction requests for requesters.
  • Settings - The administrator settings views.
    • Permissions - The overview for all actions and which roles are permitted to perform them.
    • Course Management - The list of all courses which can be linked to correction requests. Courses can be added, deleted and modified within this view.
    • User Management - The list of all users which do currently have an account for the system with their role. Users can modified within this view.
  • Privacy Policy - The legal document discribing the use of personal data on this site.
  • About Us - The view containing information about the website creator and owners.

You can also modify your profile, log out or change your password by using the menu on the right upper corner and clicking on your username.
